Instructions to play:

Moving Around

Use the arrow keys to move the Gnome around.

Swapping Proteins

Treating genetic mutation is easy! When the Gnome touches a protein, it swaps with the protein above.

Sort the Proteins

Rearrange the proteins in increasing order from top to bottom to save the child from the genetic disorder.

About Gnome Sort

This sorting process you are simulatimg is known as a Gnome Sort, sometimes referred to as "Stupid Sort". The idea is that a garden gnome wants to sort his flower pots by looking at the flower pot next to him and comparing to the previous pot. If they are in the correct order, he moves on; if not, he swaps it with the previous pot.

Read more about it here: https://www.geeksforgeeks.org/gnome-sort-a-stupid-one/
